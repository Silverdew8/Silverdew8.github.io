My name is Jingyi Yuan, and I am currently studying at Syracuse University. My hometown is Yantai, Shandong province, China. Yantai is not the top city like Beijing and Shanghai in China, but it is my favorite city. People in Yantai love seafood because Yantai is a coastal city, and the seafood in Yantai is much cheaper and fresh than other cities. Also, I enjoy the peace of the town. Every morning I wake up in Yantai, the first thing come into my eyes is the coastal and the sea. I will usually then take the breakfast with me and walk along the seaside.
